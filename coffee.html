Best Coffee in San Jose

Phin Cafe

Phin Cafe is located in South San Jose, CA. Currently, there is only one location in CA. This cafe is known for their egg coffee, which is a Vietnamese coffee base with a sweet foam on top made of egg yolks and sweetened condensed milk. Egg coffee can be served iced or hot and pairs well with a Bánh mì.

7Leaves Cafe

7Leaves's first location was in Little Saigon of Orange County, CA. Ever since then, there has been 43 total stores located across California, Nevada, Texas, and Atlanta. Aside from their delicious boba drink, 7Leaves is well known for their House coffee, which is Vietnamese Iced Coffee topped with a salty, sweet cream foam.
